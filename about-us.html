Our Story

We are a team of professional sea lovers. For 5 years now we have been gathering groups of tourists and going to amazing yacht trips around the most beautiful corners of the planet. Thanks to carefully thought-out tours, you have nothing to worry about: you relax, expose your face to the salty wind, swim in the clear sea and spend a splendid vacation in pleasant multinational company.


We have own fleet of modern vessels and proficient crew. That is, we don't rent yachts or hire staff for tours but have our own capacities. We provide all the services by our own and with help of our selected partners. YachtSail offers luxury yachting experience affordable to anyone. See you on board!

Our Core Values are:
  • Maximum safety for tourists
  • High-quality services
  • Uncommon spectacular places
  • Feeling at a yacht like at home
  • Affordable voyages
  • Unforgettable moments
Our Promises
Exceptional experience
Sailing on a yacht is much different from anything you've experienced before. It's all about salty wind, turquoise water and white beaches. You will fall asleep under the measured rocking of the yacht on the waves and have breakfast with the most spectacular views!
Perfect service during tours
All our trips are accompanied by professional guides and hosts. Our crew will make everything possible for your comfort and satisfaction, no matter where you go, which type of yacht and which package you choose. From captain-only to all inclusive options we care for our guests.
Clear booking options
When booking a trip, our managers clears all the options included in your payment. We have 3 different options for cabin booking. Also, you can rent the entire yacht with no strangers on it except for the crew. The price doesn't include gratuity on which you decide by your own.

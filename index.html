From €650 p.p.

Croatia is an amazing country, with almost untouched nature, incredibly beautiful harbors and small islands. We are going on a 7-day voyage on these islands! Bathing in the open sea, contemplation of the wonderful nature, green woods, sandy beaches and red tile roofs of ancient houses are waiting for you. We start from Pula and go through the islands of Krk, Hvar, Cres, Rab, Mljet and 10 more islands!

Vacation Around Amalfi

  • JUN
  • JUL
  • AUG
  • SEP

From €800 p.p.

The majestic and legendary Amalfi Coast is surrounded by historical charm and scenic romanticism, this is the best place for yachting with its emerald waters. Swimming and snorkeling, the archaeological ruins, the wonders of local cuisine and much more are waiting for you. The gentle sea breeze makes the rays of the sun soft, and the climate is ideal for your summer holidays.

Through the Fjords of Norway

  • JUN
  • JUL
  • AUG
  • SEP

From €900 p.p.

For 7 days of sailing, you will see what the country of the Vikings is so wonderful about. We will go through the fjords, stopping in local villages, where we’ll eat crabs, stroll through picturesque Bergen, see the colorful Roosendaal gardens, admire the fjords abyss from the top of the mountain, spend a night in a colorful fishing village. And of course we will go fishing for trout and salmon.

Non-touristic Turkey

  • JUN
  • JUL
  • AUG
  • SEP

From €550 p.p.

Seven days of yachting will help you truly discover another Turkey. Every day will be different from the previous one - incredibly picturesque coasts, cozy bays, ancient cities, original non-touristic flavor with hospitable people. The lovers of scuba diving will be surprised by the diverse marine world - there are sea urchins with unrealistically long needles, colorful fish, sea turtles and more.

Ancient Greece

  • JUN
  • JUL
  • AUG
  • SEP

From €750 p.p.

Greece is not only about Athens or Halkidiki. This country has a great history and islands of amazing beauty. We offer a 10-day trip to the most spectacular places in Greece, with docking at ports and exciting excursions. You will see the islands of Zakynthos, Aegina, Methana, Paros, Hydra, Spetses and more. Amazing adventures, diving and even fishing at will are waiting for you!

Spain + Nightlife at Ibiza

  • JUN
  • JUL
  • AUG
  • SEP

From €900 p.p.

We leave from Valencia, then travel 7 days along Costa Blanca (visiting the cities of Javea, Gandia, Denia, Calpe, Althea) and return to Valencia. In addition, we will stay for 2 nights in Ibiza, where you can dance all night in the most famous nightclubs - Pacha, Privilege, Space, Es Paradis or any other at your choice. You can admire amazing landscapes, turquoise sea and parties every day!

Our Story

We are a team of professional sea lovers. For 5 years now we have been gathering groups of tourists and going to amazing yacht trips around the most beautiful corners of the planet. Thanks to carefully thought-out tours, you have nothing to worry about: you relax, expose your face to the salty wind, swim in the clear sea and spend a splendid vacation in pleasant multinational company.


We have own fleet of modern vessels and proficient crew. That is, we don't rent yachts or hire staff for tours but have our own capacities.

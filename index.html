The majestic and legendary Amalfi Coast is surrounded by historical charm and scenic romanticism, this is the best place for yachting with its emerald waters. Accommodation Options

Cabin

All cabins are fully-equipped with everything necessary for relaxing after a day full of impressions. The cabins are designed to host 2 people + 1 child, some of the cabins have a seating area. We have twin and double cabins, you can choose the preferred option when booking a trip.

  • Private bathrooms;
  • Kettle and filled mini-bar;
  • Twin or double at your choice.

Yacht

If you travel with your friends, you can rent the whole yacht to join us at any of the proposed trips. Our yachts can host up to 12 people + kids. The prices depend on the type of the yacht you book. Whatever your choice is, you get the modern, fully-equipped yacht with professional crew.

  • No strangers on your yacht;
  • Private bathroom in every cabin;
  • Fully-equipped kitchen and lounge zone.

Crew Services

Captain-only

If you are ready to learn yachting, moor the vessel, tie knots and much more, then this option is for you! A proficient and charismatic captain will teach you yachting and tell stories about sailing.

Captain & Skipper Included

Skipper and captain will take care of everything connected with the yacht. Docking, sailing, knots tying - you don't need to think about it. Just have fun on the yacht, sunbathe and swim in the harbors.

All Inclusive

Don't want to lift a finger? This is the best option for you. It includes a captain, skipper, cook, waiters and even housemaids to make you trip a really luxury experience. Get aboard and enjoy your vacation!

Payment Options

10%, 30%, 60%

There's no need to pay the full price the day you book your yacht vacation. You can split the sum and pay in 3-5 instalments. You can discuss the options with our manager when booking the trip.

Early booking

If you want to book the whole yacht, then do it in advance as usually, by the beginning of the season, there are only few cabins left on each yacht. So, we advice you make early booking, plus saving with the special offers.

Our Story
